Permutation of the Last Layer (PLL): Simple Cases

Edges Only

Ua Perm: M2’ U M U2’ M’ U M2’

Ub Perm: M2’ U’ M U2’ M’ U’ M2’

H Perm: M2’ U M2’ U2 M2’ U M2’

Z Perm: M’ U’ M2’ U’ M2’ U’ M’ U2’ M2’ U


Adjacent Corner Swap

Aa Perm; x (R’ U R') D2 (R U' R') D2 R2

Ab Perm: x R2 D2 (R U R') D2 (R U' R)

T Perm: (R U R' U') R' F (R2 U' R' U') (R U R' F')

F Perm: R' U' F' (R U R' U') R' F (R2 U' R' U') (R U) (R' U R)

Ja Perm: x (R2 F R F’) R U2 (r’ U r) U2

Jb Perm: (R U R’ F’) (R U R’ U’) R’ F R2 U’ R’ U’

Ra Perm: (R U R’ F’) (R U2’ R’ U2’) (R’ F R U) (R U2’ R’ U’)

Rb Perm: R2 F R (U R U’ R’) F’ R U2 R’ U2 R U


Diagonal Corner Swap

E Perm: x’ (R U' R' D) (R U R' D') (R U R' D) (R U' R' D')

V Perm: R' U R U' R' f' U' (R U2 R' U' R U' R') f R

Y Perm: F (R U’ R’ U’) (R U R’ F’) (R U R’ U’) (R’ F R F’)

Na Perm: (R U R' U) (R U R' F') (R U R' U') R' F (R2 U' R') (U2 R U' R')

Nb Perm: r’ D’ F (r U’ r’) F’ D (r2 U r’ U’) (r’ F r F’)
